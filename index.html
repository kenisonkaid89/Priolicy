Privacy Policy

Effective Date: September 27, 2024

Thank you for choosing Britty's Savings Plan. Your privacy is critically important to us, and we are committed to safeguarding your data. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data, particularly that we do not collect or store any personal or user-generated data.

1. Information We Do Not Collect

No Personal Information: We do not collect or request any personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. This application is designed with your privacy in mind, ensuring that your personal details are never stored or shared.

No Device Information: We do not collect any device-related data. This includes but is not limited to device identifiers, IP addresses, or system information. Your use of this app is entirely private.

2. User-Generated Data

No Data Storage: Any data you generate while using Britty's Savings Plan, including savings entries or financial information, is stored locally on your device only. We do not access, store, or transmit this data. Your records are strictly under your control and never leave your device.

3. Security of Your Data

Data Security: Since we do not collect or store any of your data, there is no risk of data breaches or leaks from our side. We strongly encourage users to take appropriate security measures on their devices, such as enabling encryption or locking features, to safeguard their data stored locally.

4. No Third-Party Services

No External Services: This application does not integrate with third-party services such as analytics providers, advertising networks, or data brokers. We do not share or transmit any data to external servers. You can rest assured that no third parties have access to any data related to your use of the app.

5. Cookies and Tracking

No Cookies: This application does not use cookies, trackers, or similar technologies. Your activities within the app are not monitored, recorded, or analyzed in any way.

6. User Consent

By using Britty's Savings Plan, you acknowledge and agree to this Privacy Policy. Since we do not collect data, you can be confident that your privacy is fully respected and preserved while using the app.

7. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy periodically to reflect any necessary changes in our practices or for legal reasons. Any updates will be posted within the app, and the effective date will be revised accordingly. We encourage users to review this page regularly to stay informed of any changes.

9. Data Retention Policy

No Data Retention: As we do not collect or store any user data, we also do not retain any information related to your use of the app. All data remains on your device until you choose to delete it.

10. Children's Privacy

Not Intended for Children: While Britty's Savings Plan is designed to be used by a general audience, we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13. Since we do not collect data, there are no specific risks for children. However, if a parent or guardian becomes aware that their child is using the app and they have concerns, they may contact us to discuss any questions regarding the app's functionality.

11. User Responsibility

User Responsibility: While we ensure that no data is collected or shared through the app, users are encouraged to secure their devices by using strong passwords, enabling device encryption, and regularly updating their software. This helps protect any information stored locally on their device from unauthorized access.

12. Data Deletion

Deleting Data: Any data you generate while using Britty's Savings Plan remains stored only on your device. To delete this data, you may uninstall the application or manually clear the app's storage through your device's settings. Since no data is stored externally, this process will fully remove all saved information.
